**About Me**

I am a second year Ph.D. student at the [<span style="color:blue">Department of Computer Science</span>](http://www.cs.ox.ac.uk), [<span style="color:blue">University of Oxford</span>](http://www.ox.ac.uk). My research focuses on the interaction among machine learning, computer vision, optimization, and quantum computing.
